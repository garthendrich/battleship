Battleship

How to play

Battleship is a board game where two players playing against each other will attempt to guess the locations of the other players's ships and sink them.

The first to sink all their opponent's ships wins.

  • Carrier
    Hold a ship and drag it onto the board
  • Battleship
  • Destroyer
  • Submarine
  • Patrol Boat
